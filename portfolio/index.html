Welcome

I’ve been passionate about game development since childhood—always more curious about how games were built than just playing them. This curiosity sparked a lifelong fascination with interactive systems and gameplay mechanics, driving me to explore programming deeply and experiment with creating immersive experiences.

Today, I work as a Lead Tools Engineer, building cross-platform pipelines and tools for creative teams, while continuing to contribute to indie projects and gameplay systems that reflect that lifelong passion. I focus on the intersection of tools and gameplay—building scalable systems prioritizing performance, modularity, and extensibility across all gameplay layers.
